Full-time Missionary Suites

At Temple Inn & Suites our goal is to provide accomidations that don't distract from the spirit of serving in the house of the Lord. We have three main suites to choose from to best suit your needs!
*All of the suites provided come with in house room service, cleaning, and controlable AC/Heat*

Single Bed Suite

These charming suites provide everything you need to have a fantasic stay, including a walk in closet, personal safe, and a beautiful, spacious bathroom.

Single Queen Bed Suite

A fantasic suite with a single queen sized bed if you're looking for something bigger than our regular sized bed. This room comes with all accomidations but with more space and our personal favorite for couples!

Two Queen Beds Suite

This suite features two seperate queen sized beds, great for families or groups. With all accomidations included, what makes this suite special is the additional hottub and an increased kitchen size with a marble island finish.
